How to Donate

Simple steps to make an impact.

1. Collect

Gather gently used or damaged pickleballs from your team, school, or family.

2. Drop Off

Bring them to one of our collection bins or request a pickup from our volunteer team.

3. Transform

We clean, sort, and turn your donated balls into new community products and educational tools.
