About Second Serve

We give used pickleballs a second life.

Second Serve is a student-led nonprofit that collects used pickleballs and transforms them into new products for schools, shelters, and community groups.

Mission

Reduce waste while helping schools, shelters, and the community through creative reuse.

Values

  • Sustainability
  • Creativity
  • Community
  • Education

What We Do

Reuse projects that inspire students and families.

🐾

Dog Treat Puzzles

Convert pickleballs into playful treat puzzles for pets and shelters.

🌱

Seed Starters

Turn pickleballs into lightweight starter pots for school garden projects.

🏫

PE Class Reuse

Support physical education with recycled gear and creative lesson resources.

🎨

Painted Ornaments

Design colorful community art and ornaments from reused balls.

📱

Phone Stands

Craft easy phone stands from repurposed pickleball shells.

🔌

Cable Organizers

Create tidy tech helpers from everyday recycled materials.

💚

Fundraising Projects

Host events that support student groups and community causes.
