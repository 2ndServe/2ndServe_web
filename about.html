About Us

Students turning pickleball waste into community good.

Second Serve is a student-led nonprofit collecting used pickleballs and converting them into useful products for schools, shelters, and local partners.

Contact Our Team

Our Mission

Reduce waste while helping communities learn and grow.

We collect gently used pickleballs and give them a second purpose through creative reuse initiatives, student education, and local outreach.

Sustainability

Every ball we rescue means fewer materials in the landfill.

Creativity

Students design new solutions using everyday recycled materials.

Community

We build stronger neighborhoods through shared purpose and reuse.

Education

Our projects teach environmental stewardship and hands-on skills.

Why It Matters

Small actions, big community benefits.

Pickleballs are made from durable materials that can stay in landfills for years. By collecting and repurposing them, we turn waste into resources and create educational projects that support schools and families.

Second Serve makes reuse accessible, fun, and meaningful — one donated ball at a time.
